About Founder

Akhilesh Kumar

Hello, I am Akhilesh Kumar from Greater Noida, a veteran Educator and senior Mentoria Career Counselor with over 30 years teaching experience. I have had the privilege of serving in several leadership and senior academic roles across reputed institutions. I have headed the Mathematics Departments at Somerville International School, Noida; Greater Valley School, Greater Noida; and Holy Angel's Sr. Sec. School, Ghaziabad. Over the years, I have also contributed as a PGT Mathematics at Gaurs International School, Greater Noida West, and Ch. Chhabil Dass Public School, Ghaziabad, as well as a Mathematics Faculty at THE CORE Educational Solutions in Delhi. In addition to my school roles, I have served as a CBSE Head Examiner and Evaluation Coordinator for Mathematics, overseeing the evaluation process at CBSE centers.

Success Stories

From Math Anxiety to Academic Mastery

One of the most rewarding journeys involved a student at Greater Valley School who consistently struggled with the fear of numbers. By shifting the focus from rote memorization to conceptual depth, we moved past the anxiety. Within a single academic year, the student transitioned from failing grades to scoring a distinction in their CBSE Boards. Today, that same student is pursuing a successful career, proving that once a student gains Clarity, success is inevitable.

Path to Ivy-League Level Excellence

While heading the Mathematics department at Somerville International, I mentored a bright mind who excelled in calculations but lacked the strategic direction for global competition. Through a blend of academic rigor and personalized career guidance, we mapped out a path that balanced school priorities with international requirements. This student eventually secured a scholarship to a top-tier university, attributing their success to the Vedanta principle of focused, disciplined preparation.

Navigating Career Crossroads

A student at Ch. Chhabil Dass Public School was under immense pressure to follow a traditional engineering path despite having a profound aptitude for design. Through the focused framework and deep counseling, I helped both the student and the parents see the crystal clear potential in an alternative field. By bridging the gap between innate strengths and academic choices, the student successfully pursued Architecture and is now a leading professional in the NCR region, truly living the ClariVeda mission.
